# Introduction

This document defines the core operational workflows of AstraNova Global.

Each workflow represents a business process that generates enterprise data. These business events become the foundation for ETL pipelines, warehouse tables, analytics, and dashboards within HELIX.

---

# Core Business Processes

HELIX models the following enterprise workflows:

1. Customer Lifecycle
2. Sales Process
3. Inventory Management
4. Procurement Process
5. Warehouse Operations
6. Shipping & Logistics
7. Product Returns
8. Customer Support
9. Marketing Campaigns
10. Finance
11. Human Resources

---

# Business Event Principle

Every workflow generates business events.

Business Events become transactional data.

Transactional data enters ETL.

ETL loads the Enterprise Warehouse.

The warehouse powers analytics.

Analytics generates business intelligence.
