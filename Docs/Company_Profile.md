# Company Overview

AstraNova Global is a multinational consumer electronics and smart technology company specializing in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of innovative products for homes, businesses, and enterprise customers.

The company operates across multiple global regions with integrated business functions including Sales, Finance, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, Human Resources, Marketing, Customer Support, and Data & Analytics.

As AstraNova expanded internationally, different departments adopted independent software systems and reporting processes. While each department optimized its own operations, the lack of centralized data created fragmented reporting, inconsistent business metrics, and slower executive decision-making.

To address these challenges, AstraNova initiated **Project HELIX**, an enterprise-wide Data Intelligence Platform designed to unify organizational data, automate analytics, and provide trusted insights for every level of decision-making.

# Why HELIX Exists

As AstraNova continued to grow, business data became distributed across multiple enterprise systems.

This resulted in:

- Multiple versions of the same KPI
- Manual Excel reporting
- Slow executive reporting
- Limited visibility across departments
- Delayed operational decisions
- Data quality inconsistencies

HELIX was created to eliminate these challenges by establishing a centralized enterprise data platform capable of integrating data, automating analytics, and enabling real-time business intelligence across the organization.
