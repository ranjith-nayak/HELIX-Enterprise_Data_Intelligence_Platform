# Introduction

HELIX is designed to support multiple business stakeholders across AstraNova Global. Each stakeholder has unique responsibilities, business goals, and analytical requirements.

This document identifies the primary users of HELIX and defines the information each stakeholder needs to make informed business decisions.

---

- Company performance
