# Introduction

HELIX is designed as a modular Enterprise Data Intelligence Platform.

Each module serves a specific business function while sharing a centralized enterprise data warehouse.

Every module has its own KPIs, dashboards, reports, AI insights, and business workflows.

---

# Module Overview

| Module | Primary Users | Purpose |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| Executive Command Center | CEO, Executives | Enterprise Overview |
| Sales Intelligence | Sales Teams | Revenue & Sales Analytics |
| Finance Intelligence | Finance | Financial Performance |
| Customer Intelligence | Marketing & Sales | Customer Behavior |
| Supply Chain Intelligence | Operations | Inventory & Logistics |
| HR Intelligence | HR | Workforce Analytics |
| Marketing Intelligence | Marketing | Campaign Analytics |
| Customer Support Intelligence | Support Team | Service Performance |
| Data Operations Center | Data Engineers | Platform Monitoring |
| AI Intelligence Engine | All Users | Smart Insights & Predictions |

# Module Relationships

Executive Command Center receives summarized insights from every business module.

The AI Intelligence Engine enhances every module by generating intelligent insights, forecasts, and recommendations.

The Data Operations Center supports all modules by ensuring reliable and trusted data.
